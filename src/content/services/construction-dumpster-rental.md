## Built for the Job Site

Whether you're tearing off a roof, pouring a foundation, or gutting an interior, construction generates serious waste. Waste Falcon delivers the right dumpster to your job site and picks it up when you're done — so your crew can focus on building, not hauling.

### Who We Serve

- **Roofing companies** — shingle tear-offs, metal roofing debris
- **General contractors** — new construction, remodels, additions
- **Concrete & masonry** — broken concrete, brick, block
- **Demolition crews** — interior and exterior demo debris

### Dumpster Sizes for Construction

| Size | Best For | Weight Limit |
|------|----------|-------------|
| 10 Yard | Small remodels, single-room tear-outs | ~2 tons |
| 15 Yard | Roof tear-offs (up to 25 squares) | ~3 tons |
| 20 Yard | Medium construction projects | ~4 tons |
| 30 Yard | Large remodels, new construction | ~5 tons |
| 40 Yard | Major construction, commercial builds | ~6 tons |

1. **Call or request a quote** — tell us your project type and timeline
