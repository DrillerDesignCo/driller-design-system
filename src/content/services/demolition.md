## Demolition Done Right

Demolition is more than swinging a sledgehammer. It takes planning, the right equipment, and responsible debris disposal. Waste Falcon handles the full scope — from selective interior demo to complete structure teardown — with our own dumpsters on-site for seamless waste removal.

### Our Demolition Services

- **Interior demolition** — walls, ceilings, flooring, fixtures, cabinets
- **Exterior demolition** — sheds, garages, decks, fences, concrete
- **Selective/surgical demo** — remove specific elements while preserving the structure
- **Commercial demolition** — tenant buildout removal, warehouse clearing
- **Site preparation** — clearing for new construction or renovation

Other demo contractors have to rent dumpsters. **We own ours.** That means:

- **No waiting for dumpster delivery** — it arrives with our crew
- **Lower cost** — no third-party dumpster rental markup
