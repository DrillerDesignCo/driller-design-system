## Move-Out Season, Handled

Every August and May, College Station apartments face the same problem: mountains of furniture, mattresses, and junk left behind by departing students. Waste Falcon works with property managers to place dumpsters before the rush hits.

### Who This Is For

- **Property managers** — multi-unit complexes, student housing
- **Apartment management companies** — scheduled turnover support
- **HOAs & condo associations** — community cleanout events
- **Universities & student orgs** — move-in/move-out drives

### Why Property Managers Choose Us

- **Pre-scheduled delivery** — dumpsters placed before move-out day
- **Multiple units** — we can place several dumpsters across your property
- **Quick swap** — when one fills up, we swap it same-day
- **Bulk pricing** — seasonal contracts for repeat clients
